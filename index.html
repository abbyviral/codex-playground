Plane Figures (Area)

Square (Square = a²)

Ek square mein sab sides equal. Area milta hai side × side. Exam mein units pe dhyaan rakho!

A = a²
Example: Side = 6 cm ⇒ A = 36 cm²
Mistake alert: Perimeter formula (4a) ko area samajh ke apply mat karna.
Sample Problems
  1. Side 12 cm ka square ka area? (144 cm²)
  2. Class board 1.2 m side — kitna paint chahiye agar 1 litre = 10 m² cover kare?

Rectangle (Area = l × b)

Length aur breadth alag ho sakte. Area simple multiplication hai. Formula revise karo jab bhi desks measure karo.

A = l × b
Example: 8 cm × 5 cm ⇒ 40 cm²
Common doubt: Diagonal nahi chahiye area ke liye.
Sample Problems
  1. Garden 20 m × 15 m ka area aur fencing length find karo.
  2. Notebook cover 18 cm × 12 cm, plastic sheet kitna area cover karega?

Triangle (½ × b × h & Heron)

Triangle ka area base aur height se ya phir Heron's se jab sides pata ho. Semi-perimeter s ka dhyaan rakho.

A = ½ × b × h
s = (a + b + c)/2
A = √(s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c))
Example: b = 12 cm, h = 7 cm ⇒ A = 42 cm²
Tip: Height hamesha base par perpendicular hoti hai.
Sample Problems
  1. Sides 13 cm, 14 cm, 15 cm ⇒ area? (Use Heron)
  2. Triangle base 20 cm, area 90 cm² ⇒ height?

Parallelogram (Area = b × h)

Opposite sides parallel. Area bas base aur perpendicular height se decide hota.

A = b × h
Example: b = 10 cm, h = 6 cm ⇒ 60 cm²
Mistake: Slant side ko height mat samjho.
  1. Base 18 cm, area 108 cm² ⇒ height?
  2. Parallelogram formed by vectors, base double hone par area?

Trapezium (Average of parallel sides)

Do parallel sides a aur b ka average leke height se multiply karo.

A = ½ × (a + b) × h
Example: a=10 cm, b=6 cm, h=5 cm ⇒ 40 cm²
Tip: Parallel sides ka difference confuse mat kare.
  1. Field boundary shaped trapezium: find area.
  2. Find missing parallel side when area aur height given.

Circle (Area = πr²)

Circle ko pizza ki tarah slices mein cut karo, rectangle ban jaata — isi se area samjho.

A = πr², π ≈ 3.14
Example: r = 7 cm ⇒ A ≈ 153.86 cm²
Common mistake: Diameter ko radius mat samjho. Radius half hota hai.
  1. Wheel radius 0.35 m ⇒ area?
  2. Find radius if area 201 cm².

3D Solids (Surface Area & Volume)

Cube

Rubik's cube jaisa. Sab edges same.

TSA = 6a²
LSA = 4a²
Volume = a³

Example: a = 5 cm ⇒ TSA = 150 cm², V = 125 cm³

Cuboid

Pencil box ya book box jaisa.

TSA = 2(lb + bh + hl)
LSA = 2h(l + b)
V = lbh

Example: 4 × 3 × 2 cm ⇒ TSA = 52 cm², V = 24 cm³

Cylinder

Water tank, batteries, dabbas — sab cylinder type.

CSA = 2πrh
TSA = 2πrh + 2πr²
Volume = πr²h

Example: r = 3 cm, h = 10 cm ⇒ CSA ≈ 188.4 cm², V ≈ 282.6 cm³
