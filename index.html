Meet Rahul Yadav

AI Mentor, Motivational Educator, and Founder of The Mission Development Academy.

Rahul Yadav, fondly known as Mentor Sir, is on a mission to transform education through Artificial Intelligence and future-ready skills. From designing AI-powered learning journeys to motivating audiences across India, he blends strategy, storytelling, and hands-on guidance to make AI accessible for everyone.

Having collaborated with NCERT India at Didac India 2024, partnered with Google India to empower educators with AI tools, and inspired 5000+ participants at TIT College, Rahul is at the forefront of India’s AI-in-Education movement. His workshops demystify AI, break career barriers, and energise institutions to embrace innovation.

Over the next five years, Rahul envisions establishing himself as the go-to AI Mentor for students, professionals, and organisations. He is building The Mission Development Academy into an AI-powered educational hub where learners dive into AI tools, skill development, and future-ready learning experiences.

Vision 2025 – 2030

  • Deliver AI trainings, workshops, and seminars across India.
  • Equip educators and leaders with actionable AI playbooks.
  • Build The Mission Development Academy into a trusted AI hub.
  • Empower every learner to thrive in an AI-first future.
